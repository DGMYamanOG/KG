About Us

Lilac Valley Stay is a simple, rustic farm stay accommodation in the hills of the Blue Mountains. The property is owned by Marina Ye, a local interior designer who purchased the old rundown barn three years ago with a plan to renovate. After years of hard work, the property now features a beautiful restored, open plan, luxury barn house on four acres of land home to sheep, chickens, ducks and one very friendly goat named rosie.

There is an outdoor plunge pool just off the back verandah and a stunning cottage garden. The Lilac Valley Farm Stay has been incredibly popular on AirBnB, and was even featured in a handful of online articles.
